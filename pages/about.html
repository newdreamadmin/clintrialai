About ClinTrialAI

We are reimagining clinical trials by harnessing the power of artificial intelligence to create scalable, efficient, and patient-centric trial infrastructure.

Our Mission

To accelerate drug development and bring life-saving therapies to patients faster by converting the most unpredictable component of pharmaceutical R&D into scalable, software-driven infrastructure powered by artificial intelligence.

Our Vision

A future where clinical trials are no longer the bottleneck in drug development. Where AI-powered systems enable seamless patient identification, enrollment, monitoring, and data collection, allowing researchers to focus on what matters most: advancing medical science.

Why ClinTrialAI Exists

The Problem

80% of clinical trials fail to complete on time. Patient recruitment is the single most rate-limiting step, consuming 30% of trial budgets. Manual processes, fragmented workflows, and EHR systems not designed for trial operations create inefficiencies at every stage.

The Opportunity

90%+ EHR adoption provides unprecedented access to patient data. Advanced AI makes complex eligibility screening computationally feasible. Healthcare organizations face budget pressure demanding operational efficiency. The market is ready for transformation.

Our Solution

An end-to-end AI platform integrating with existing EHRs to automate patient identification, enrollment, screening, randomization, monitoring, and data collection. Converting unpredictable trial timelines into predictable software-driven processes.
