What is ClinTrialAI?

An end-to-end, AI-enabled clinical trial execution platform that integrates with EHRs while operating independently of them.

Recruit

Continuous real-time pre-screening and identification of eligible patients using complex, multi-dimensional criteria

Screen

Automated eligibility verification across EHR data — labs, imaging, medications, and other unstructured data

Randomize

Built-in protocol-driven randomization with full audit trail and compliance

Follow-Up

Longitudinal monitoring, automated scheduling, enhanced adherence, real-time data capture and automated uploading of CRFs

Close

Streamlined data lock, regulatory submission, and trial close-out reporting
